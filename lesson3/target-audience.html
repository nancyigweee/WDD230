Target Audience

Anyone looking to market their products in the Rexburg area.

Personas

1. Susan - Owns a jewelry store downtown Rexburg. She sells only 24kt gold and welcomes customers looking for great quality jewelry at affordable prices. 2. Mike - Owns a local produce business and is always looking for customers that love supporting local businesses and eating healthy.

Scenarios

1. Where can I get local produce? I'm tired of buying frozen fruits and veggies. 2. I keep eating the same food. I would like to find new restaurants. 3. Are there any hair salons nearby?? 4. Where can I find vegan food in Rexburg? 5. I'm getting married soon and would love to invest in good quality jewelry. Where can I buy these nearby?
